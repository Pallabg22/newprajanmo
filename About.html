About Us

Learn more about New Prajanmo

Our Mission

New Prajanmo is a non-profit organization working to uplift underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and social empowerment initiatives.

Our Vision

To build a society where everyone has access to basic needs, equal opportunities, and a platform to grow and contribute positively to the world.

Our Team

Our dedicated team consists of passionate volunteers, social workers, educators, and health professionals working together to bring impactful changes in rural and urban areas.
